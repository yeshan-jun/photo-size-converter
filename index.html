Helpful answers

Photo size converter FAQ

Does the result stay below my target size?

Yes. The converter searches for the clearest JPEG result that remains at or below the target KB you enter.

How does the tool keep the photo clear?

It first searches JPEG quality and only reduces image dimensions when the selected target requires a smaller file.

Will phone photos display in the correct direction?

Supported browsers apply the photo orientation stored by iPhone and Android cameras before compression.

Are transparent PNG files supported?

Yes. Transparent areas are placed on a white background because the downloadable result uses the JPEG format.
